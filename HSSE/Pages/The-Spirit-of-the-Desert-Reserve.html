​The Spirit of the Desert Reserve
Kuwait Oil Company inaugurated the “Spirit of the Desert” reserve, located in Burgan Oil Field, on November 6, 2006 in order to provide a safe haven for local and migrant birds and wild plants, and to revive wildlife in the area in the framework of the Company’s efforts to combat desertification through restoring vegetation and soil stabilization. This project resulted in the treatment and rehabilitation of more than 240,000 square meters of land, surrounded by a water shed with an area of 67,614 square meters.
