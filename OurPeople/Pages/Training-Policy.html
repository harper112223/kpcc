​ Our Training Policy

One of the major achievements of the Corporation in terms of training and developing staff capabilities in the sector is the expansion of the Petroleum Training Centre (PTC) at Ahmadi Governorate. KPC supports the role of the national labor force at all levels. It encourages personal initiatives for education and development in the oil sector. The Council formulates training policies and plans, and reviews them annually. It also devises the general strategy for training for the oil sector. The Supreme Council for Training and Education was established to supervise the implementation of training and career structures designed for all oil-sector employees.
